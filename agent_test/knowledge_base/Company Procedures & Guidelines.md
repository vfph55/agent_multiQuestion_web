# **ZURU Melon: Company Procedures & Guidelines**

**Date:** 2025-29-10  
**Document Version:** 1.1  
**Scope:** All ZURU Melon employees, applicants, and contractors

---

## **1. Introduction**

ZURU Melon leverages advanced artificial intelligence to craft innovative solutions tailored to each client’s unique needs. These policies ensure clarity, consistency, and fairness throughout the organization.

---

## **2. Recruitment, Hiring & Selection**

**2.1. Job Posting & Applications**  
- **Step 1:** Open positions are listed on the ZURU Melon Careers Portal and major job boards.  
- **Step 2:** Candidates submit applications online, including a resume and cover letter.

**2.2. Pre-screening**  
- The HR team reviews applications within 7 business days.
- Basic qualifications and alignment with ZURU Melon’s values are assessed first.

**2.3. Interviews**  
- **Stage 1:** Phone interview covering background and motivation.
- **Stage 2:** Technical/Case Study (for technical roles), or Skills Assessment (for support roles).
- **Stage 3:** Panel interview focusing on culture fit, creativity, and problem-solving.

**2.4. Selection**  
- Panel scores are compiled, and the candidate with the highest overall alignment receives an offer contingent on reference checks.

**2.5. Offer & Acceptance**  
- Formal offers are sent via email, detailing compensation, benefits, and start date.
- Acceptance must be returned within 5 business days.

---

## **3. Onboarding**

**3.1. Welcome Kit**  
- New hires receive a digital welcome pack with handbook, AI usage guides, company values, and login credentials for company systems.

**3.2. Orientation**  
- Scheduled within the first week:
  - Introduction to the team and mentor assignment
  - AI systems and data privacy training
  - Health & safety briefing

**3.3. 30-60-90 Day Plan**  
- Defined objectives for first 30, 60, and 90 days
- Regular check-ins with mentor and supervisor

---

## **4. Working Hours, Vacations, and Leave Requests**

**4.1. Working Hours**  
- Normal hours: 9:00 AM–5:30 PM, Monday to Friday  
- Flexible/remote options available upon manager approval.

**4.2. Vacation Policy**  
- Standard: 20 paid leave days per calendar year (+ public holidays)
- Leave year: Jan 1–Dec 31

**4.3. Leave Request Process**  
1. Submit request through the HR Portal at least 2 weeks in advance.
2. Manager reviews and approves within 5 business days.
3. HR updates the leave record system and confirms via email.

**4.4. Sick Leave**  
- Notify via HR Portal or phone as soon as possible, ideally before shift begins.
- A doctor’s note is required for absences beyond 3 consecutive days.

---

## **5. AI Project Procedure**

**5.1. Client Onboarding**  
- Project Manager (PM) holds discovery call with client.
- Needs assessment conducted.
- Proposal with AI solution details, timeline, and cost sent for approval.

**5.2. Solution Design & Delivery**  
- Cross-functional team develops proof-of-concept (POC).
- Regular reviews and feedback cycles with client.
- Performance metrics and data privacy compliance checked at each stage.

**5.3. Post-Delivery Support**  
- Hand-off documentation provided.
- Client receives training.
- 30-day support window for troubleshooting.

---

## **6. Complaints and Feedback**

**6.1. Internal Complaints**  
- Employees submit grievances confidentially through the HR Portal or to hr@zurumelon.com.
- Anonymous reporting available.
- HR investigates and resolves within 10 business days.
- Appeals handled by the HR Manager and one neutral senior leader.

**6.2. Client Complaints**  
- Complaints logged via helpdesk@zurumelon.com or the Client Portal.
- Acknowledgement within 1 business day.
- Initial response/resolution provided within 3 business days.

---

## **7. Data Security and Confidentiality**

- All client and operational data is handled per GDPR and local regulations.
- Employees must use approved tools and encrypted channels for sensitive communications.
- Violations may result in disciplinary action, up to termination.

---

## **8. Ethical Use of AI**

- ZURU Melon prohibits the use of its AI systems for unlawful, discriminatory, or harmful purposes.
- Projects undergo Ethics Review Board review if there is a potential for risk or controversy.

---

## **9. Amendments & Contacts**

- Policy updates communicated via company-wide email and posted to the intranet.
- For questions, contact HR at hr@zurumelon.com or the Policy Admin at policies@zurumelon.com.

---

**ZURU Melon ensures all personnel and clients are treated with fairness, dignity, and respect, guaranteeing innovative services with ethical integrity.**
